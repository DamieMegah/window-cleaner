Save money on your window cleaning.

You can get a tax deduction for window cleaning performed your home or holiday home.

You cna get aservice deduction of up to DKK 6,600(per person over 18 years) in 2023.
The service deduction is worth approximately 26% in 2023. So if you paid DKK 1,000 for window cleaning, you save approximately DKK 260 in tax.

You can only get a deduction for the labor cost. To get a service deduction, it is a condition that you have paid electronically.
Remember to save the invoice as documentation for the work performed.
